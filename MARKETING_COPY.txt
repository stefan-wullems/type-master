Master Typing on Any Webpage

Boost your typing skills effortlessly with TypeMaster, a Chrome extension that turns any webpage into your personal typing practice playground! Whether you’re a student, professional, or typing enthusiast, TypeMaster lets you hone your speed and accuracy directly on the content you’re already reading—no extra apps or websites needed.

Why You’ll Love TypeMaster
- Practice Anywhere: Transform articles, blogs, or any selected text into a typing exercise with a single click.
- Auto-scrolling: The page automatically scrolls as you're typing so that the current text is always perfectly in view.
- Real-Time Feedback: See your progress instantly with subtle color-coded cues—green for perfect, red for errors, and orange for corrections.
- Smart Navigation: Skip filler text with Tab and move between paragraphs with enter.
- Dark Mode Ready: Seamlessly adapts to light and dark websites—always easy on the eyes.
- Accurate Stats: Track your words-per-minute (WPM) and accuracy in a sleek, draggable meter that only counts what you type.

How to use TypeMaster
- Install with one click
- Select any text on any page
- Right click and select "Start Typing Practice" or simply press Ctrl+Shift+Y
- Type each character as it’s highlighted.
- Fix mistakes with Backspace—corrected characters turn orange.
- Use Enter to move between paragraphs (look for the ⏎ symbol).
- Press Tab to skip filler section without having to type it all out.
- Track Progress: Watch your WPM and accuracy in the bottom-right corner—drag it anywhere or hide it with a click.
- Finish or Quit: Complete the text or hit Esc to exit anytime.

Perfect For
- Learners: Build typing confidence with real-world content.
- Professionals: Sharpen skills during downtime on work-related pages.
- Casual Users: Turn reading into a fun, productive challenge.
